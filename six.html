    The Fight Aganist Cancer

    Today is World Cancer Day, an observance that seeks to increase awareness of the disease, which has become an
    exceedingly important consideration in the 21st century. Although there have been numerous advancements in the
    understanding, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer—factors that should contribute to the decline of the disease—the
    number of new cases diagnosed each year has continued to increase. Yet the global cancer burden could be reduced by as
    much as 30 to 50 percent through prevention strategies, particularly in the avoidance of known risk factors, according to
    the World Health Organization.
